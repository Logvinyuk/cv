Ekaterina Logvinyuk is a Russia-born pianist and piano teacher.
Ekaterina is a laureate of All-Russian and international competitions.

Ms. Logvinyuk performs solo and as part of chamber ensembles at famous venues in Moscow.
Ekaterina has for several years been brilliantly performing in concerts of the Moscow Philharmonic of Music Schools Teachers.
Currently, she is an artist in Kuskovo youth philharmonic.
The programs of her performances are very vast and cover three and a half ages of the piano music.

These days Ekaterina learning Swift programming language and start to explore web development.
